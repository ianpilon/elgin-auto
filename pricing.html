Pricing questions

Common questions about billing and what's included.

What does $299 a month include?

Unlimited calls answered 24/7. Live appointment booking into Google Calendar or your shop scheduling tool. Automatic SMS confirmations and reminders. Multilingual support. Full call transcripts and dashboard. Customer success support. No usage limits, no per-call charges, no overage surprises.

Is there a setup fee, and what is the founding rate?

No setup fee. Setup is on us. We configure the assistant, connect it to your calendar, train the voice on your service list and policies, and run test calls with you before going live. Most shops are fully set up within 48 hours. As a founding shop, Elgin Auto pays $199/month for the first three months, then $299.

Is there a contract or commitment?

Month-to-month. Cancel anytime. No annual commitment, no early-termination fees. If it is not working for your shop, you stop paying.

How does flat-rate pricing actually work?

One monthly fee. Unlimited calls. No per-minute billing, no overage charges, no surprise invoices on a busy Monday. Your cost is the same whether you get 50 calls a week or 500.

What if my shop gets a much higher call volume than typical?

$299 covers normal shop call volume, roughly a few hundred calls per month. If you run a very high-volume operation that consistently exceeds that, we will reach out and have a conversation about a tier that makes sense for both sides. We do not surprise-bill you.

What payment methods do you accept?

Credit card (Visa, Mastercard, Amex). Billed monthly on the day you sign up. Annual billing available on request if you would prefer that.

Where does the $27K figure come from?

A small owner-run shop that already answers most calls: $250 average repair order, around 10 calls a month you can't get to, plus fewer no-shows from SMS reminders and the billable hours you stop losing to phone interruptions. It does not try to put a number on your evenings and weekends. Your numbers will be different, and we will model Elgin Auto specifically on the meeting.
