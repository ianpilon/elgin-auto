The research
5 min
The window where an after-hours caller can still be won back. Past that, they have already booked their car in elsewhere.

Speed of response is the single biggest factor in whether a caller becomes a booking.

Multiple studies of inbound phone and web inquiries (Harvard Business Review, MIT, and the Lead Response Management Study among them) have shown a sharp drop in conversion when callers are not reached quickly. Plain English: the faster you pick up, the more likely they book. Wait an hour and most of them are already at another shop.

For an auto shop, "speed of response" really means one thing: did the phone get answered. Voicemail is not a response. A callback the next morning is not a response.

The AI answers in one ring, every time. The 5-minute window never closes because there is no window to begin with.
